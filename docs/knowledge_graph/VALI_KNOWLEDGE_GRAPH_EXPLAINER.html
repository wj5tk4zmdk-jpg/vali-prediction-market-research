Contract-template normalization

POLITICALSTAT is the worked template example.

Raw contract language becomes normalized VALI primitives only after review. POLITICALSTAT is one template example; it is not assumed to apply to every market, and ambiguous mappings require human review.

<political stat>→TerminalMeasure
<value>→Threshold / strike value
above / below / exactly→ComparisonOperator
<time period>→Clear Horizon component
Source Agency→Settlement-source hierarchy
first official release→Label timing rule
revision rule→Point-in-time settlement rule
Rule 7.1 review→Settlement uncertainty flag
Template normalization is not automated legal interpretation. Contract text, public rules, and human review govern before any graph object can become freeze eligible.

Theory versus evidence

Hypotheses are frozen before results are interpreted.

Expected relationships begin as hypotheses. ValidationEvidence is added later. Frozen hypotheses must not be rewritten after outcomes are known.

Future alpha research hygiene

Useful infrastructure without overclaiming.

The graph can help future alpha research by improving speed, precision, discipline, transfer, risk control, and validation hygiene.

Speed

Faster market onboarding through reusable reviewed templates and event-family shape.

Precision

Clearer terminal-event mapping, settlement source review, and Clear Horizon discipline.

Discipline

Less keyword fishing because concepts and queries are proposed before validation.

Transfer

Reusable event-family knowledge without pretending every ticker is equivalent.

Risk control

No-trade and ambiguity flags become first-class review objects.

Validation

Frozen hypotheses are compared to later evidence without rewriting the claim.
